Meet Our

Founders

Rupal Parmar

Director

Rupal Parmar, renowned as a social entrepreneur in Gujarat's Valsad District, brings her innate aesthetic sensibility to her latest endeavor in media and films. Her dedication to community upliftment and empowerment underscores her approach to storytelling. Now, stepping into the realm of media and films, Rupal merges her passion for social change with her creative vision.

Rajesh Rana

Director

Rajesh Rana, a stalwart in Gujarati theatre, boasts a rich legacy as a playwright. As Director-Producer of a lauded TV serial on Doordarshan, he has honed his craft to perfection. With a diverse background in politics and journalism, Rajesh brings a unique perspective to his ventures. Now, venturing into cinema production, he adds another dimension to his illustrious career.

Jyotsna Rokade

Director

Jyotsna Rokade, with over 12 years in Hindi and Marathi TV and Movies as a Assistant Director, is a vital asset. Her expertise spans all aspects of production, ensuring projects resonate with audiences. A proven leader, she navigates complexities with ease, fostering creativity and delivering results. Jyotsna's collaborative approach and storytelling prowess captivate viewers, earning widespread acclaim.
